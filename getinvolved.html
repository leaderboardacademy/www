Get Involved

Leader Board Academy is an education site used to help gamers enrich their gameplay experience by delivering video based learning material. We are a small dedicated team of gamers, and can use your help to improve the community experience.

There are 2 main ways to get involved.

Post A Video Lesson

Many of us use YouTube to improve our gaming knowledge. If you find a video that could be helpful to teach others, let us know so that we may include it in a future lesson. Send the url of the link here.

Share Your Expertise

If you are a skilled gamer and want to show your tips on video, spend a few minutes to help create focused content. Schedule a video sesson with one of our content producers here.
